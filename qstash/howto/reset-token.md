Your token is used to interact with the QStash API. You need it to publish messages as well as create, read, update or delete other resources, such as URL Groups and endpoints. Resetting your token will invalidate your current token and all future requests with the old token will be rejected. To reset your token, simply click on the “Reset token” button at the bottom in the QStash UI and confirm the dialog. Afterwards you should immediately update your token in all your applications.
